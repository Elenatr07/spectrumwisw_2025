Ready Solutions

How it works?

1. Select Website

Open the website that you like for its structure, design, or functionality.

2. Fill up the form

If you love the website and want something similar, click "Buy Me Now" and fill out the form that appears.

3. Wait for us to respond

We will reach out to discuss project details, including necessary adjustments, pricing, payment options, and deadlines.

4. Sign Service Agreement

We will create a contract that outlines all project details in detail. Any additional changes after the initial agreement will be treated as extra services.

5. Template Changes

Upon receiving the advance payment, we will commence work on your project. Throughout the process, we will keep in close contact to ensure that the final result aligns with your expectations.

6. Payment & Website Handover

After completing the work and receiving payment, we will either publish your website or provide it to you as a downloadable archive.

Template Customization Guide

Our ready solutions are designed to get you online fast — but we also know you may want some adjustments. Here's what’s included with every template purchase, and what would require an additional fee.

What’s Included in Every Template (No Extra Cost)

1. Template Functionality

Everything visible in the template demo — features, sections, and layout — is included with the standard package.

2. Your Content

Content replacement is included if it doesn’t require layout changes or structural adjustments to the template.

3. Your Branding

We’ll apply your branding — including logo, color scheme, and images — at no extra cost.

4. Your Images

Replacing all template images with your own is included, as long as image sizes and placement stay the same.

5. Your Domain & Hosting

We include uploading your site to your hosting and linking it to your domain as part of the service.

Available Add-Ons (Extra Cost)

1. Advanced Customization

Changes to layout, structure, or functionality — such as adding features or sections — are available as paid add-ons.

2. Complete Branding Support

Creating or refining your brand identity — including logo, fonts, images, and messaging — is available as a paid service.

3. Animations & Effects

Custom animations or changes to existing visual effects are available as extra services at an additional cost.

4. Multilingual Setup

Adding multiple languages and configuring a language switcher is an advanced feature and available at an additional cost.

5. Long-term Support

Need help after launch? Hosting, regular updates, and support are available through our extended service plans.
